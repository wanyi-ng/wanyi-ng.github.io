"Fuck It, Fuck You. This Is Me."

2019

Fuck It, Fuck You and This Is Me are a mini-series, a letter to critics and a letter for the viewer, and a promise to myself. Fuck It, Fuck You represents me breaking my silence as an artist and creator. I am not trying to come off as someone who is defensive, disrespectful, and unable to take criticism but rather acknowledging my worth and potential as an artist, standing up for my work, and showing confidence in the work that I produce. With this, I am able to continue creating work where no one can hinder my production and my growth to push forward, improve my skills, and make more work.

This Is Me is a statement and promise to myself to always be who I amand create art that I love — for me that involves calligraphy. I am not trying to stand out nor have my own style. My style is a culmination of all the calligraphy forms that inspired me to pick up a pen and make the leap from “I want to do that, too” to teaching myself. Calligraphy has become a part of me. Sometimes I just want to sit down with a pen and ink and just write something—abstract or not, it’s art for art’s sake. This means my work sometimes does not have a deeper meaning the viewer must dissect; my goal is for the viewer to simply feel the curves and the sharp strokes of the calligraphic forms. 

Create what you want to create. Who cares if someone says it’s unoriginal? Create because your heart felt something whether it was from your mind or something you saw, someone will see your passion and acknowledge that. 
I acknowledge that.
Letter from me to you
