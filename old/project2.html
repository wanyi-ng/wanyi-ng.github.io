"Error 403"

2019

This project was an experimentation in Adobe Photoshop using images and effects. It was inspired by the idea of revolution, technology, media, and known fictional revolutionary figures in pop culture, specifically V from V for Vendetta, members of fsociety in Mr. Robot, and the robbers in La Casa de Papel. The subject I chose to depict is the character design Mr. Robot. I wanted to depict chaos and the aftermath of a social revolution; the cracks on the large screen with the masked character as the central figure demonstrate this desolation after a protest in a heavily populated and technologically advanced city like Times Square, NYC. The screen, though destroyed, has stopped on the character forever on the screen, engraving itself into your memory. In a way, this piece is a fanart and homage to the themes of resistance and chaos. 

This piece was done in Adobe Photoshop. I took a screenshot of the figure from a scene in one of the episodes of the show, and used the selection and masking tools to isolate the figure from the background. Then, I touched up the image to balance lighting issues, and added the edited background elements to simulate a glitch effect.
