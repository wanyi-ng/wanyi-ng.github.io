"Composure"

2019

Composure is also an experimentation project done in Photoshop. It is one of my most personal pieces. It focuses on the internal anxiety one feels in social settings. "When someone looks at me, what do they see? What do they think of me?" Despite all our worries, we train ourselves to keep an external composure. Because of the mask(s) and the wall(s) we build to protect ourselves, the most peculiar thing about strangers in public places is the impossibility to fully know a person's thoughts, personality, interests, worries, and stories. Everyone has their own thoughts, battles, and stories.

I was particularly inspired by the lyrics in "Noises" by Pale Waves:

What do you see when you look at me?//
I can't control my emotions lately//

I wanted to create a piece that would make the viewer question, "Is the one 'composed' winning as suggested by the title or the one internally breaking down losing composure and control of their ability to stabilize themselves in public, as depicted by the figure in the bottom right of the composition?
