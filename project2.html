Error 403

Year
2019 (Fall semester)
Category
Collage, photo manipulation in Adobe PS
Overview
Project for 2D Imaging class at CUNY City College of New York

Concept

This project was a collage experimentation in Adobe Photoshop using images and effects. It was inspired by the theme of revolution, technology and media, and known fictional revolutionary figures in pop culture, specifically from V for Vendetta, Mr. Robot, and La Casa de Papel. The subject I chose to depict is the character design from Mr. Robot. In the show, a hacker group called fsociety, led by Mr. Robot, create videos presenting their manifesto and warnings about their upcoming actions. He wears a plastic mask, top hat, tuxedo, wields a cane, and uses a voice distorting software to disguise his voice in videos. The mask was from an 80s horror movie called The Careful Massacre of the Bourgeoisie and resembles the Guy Fawkes mask.

I envisioned a scene of Mr. Robot broadcasted across screens in Times Square, NYC and thought about how I could communicate, chaos, drama, resistance, and revolution through a collage.

Process

I was moved by a moment in season one, episode three of Mr. Robot, "eps1.2_d3bug.mkv", when Mr. Robot releases a video of fsociety's demands. There was a particular moment when Mr. Robot leans towards the screen/viewer with their finger pointed. I screenshotted that scene and brought it into Photoshop, where I isolated the figure from the background and edited the colors to my liking(see below).

I added shattered glass over the foreground and warped images of screen glitches in the backgroung to illustrate what I imagined screens would look like following an fsociety raid. The screen, though physically destroyed, has stopped on the character pointing at the viewer. Forever on the screen, addressing the viewer, and engraving itself into their memory.
