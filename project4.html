well-Blend

Pet Food Company Concept

wip

Brand Name

Cats need lots of protein, moderate amount of fats, and minimum amount of carbohydrates. This necessity inspired this brand's name, "well-Blend". I wanted a name that communicated to you, the consumer, this is a brand you can trust to provide healthy and delicious ingredients in nutriciously balanced recipes for your feline companion. 

Packaging

Canned cat food have more moisture (approximately 75%) and allow cats to taste meats with their actual, unprocessed texture. Canned food can also be refrigerated after opening to prevent its content from spoiling fast. Therefore, this product is diet friendly; owners can proportion their cat's meals and save the rest for the next. 

The initial label design for the product packaging was a single solid color (yellow) with the cat logo on the far right of the brand name positioned to overlap with the letter "d" and it's tail and head facing towards the brand name to emphasize it. However, this design and the white text against the yellow background lacked legibility. The images of the raw ingredients were rethought and omitted from the design because it seemed out of place and unappealing. Because the fonts chosen have such high contrast, the thin strokes get swallowed by the background rendering small text illegible. The redesign looked to solve this issue by increasing the letter strokes.
