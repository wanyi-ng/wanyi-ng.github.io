well-Blend

Year
2019 (Fall semester)
Category
Product branding for dog food company
Overview
Project for Electronic Design class at CUNY City College of New York

Concept

Brand Name

Cats need large amounts of protein, moderate amount of fats, and minimum amount of carbohydrates. This necessity inspired this brand's name, "well-Blend". I wanted a name that communicated to you, the consumer, that this is a brand you can trust to provide healthy and delicious ingredients in nutriciously balanced recipes for your feline companion.

Packaging

Canned cat food have more moisture (approximately 75%) and allow cats to taste meats with their actual, unprocessed texture. Canned food can also be refrigerated after opening to prevent its content from spoiling fast. Therefore, this product is diet friendly; owners can proportion their cat's meals and save the rest for the next.

Process

The initial label design for the product packaging was a single solid color (yellow) with the cat logo on the far right of the brand name positioned to overlap with the letter "d" and it's tail and head facing towards the brand name to emphasize it (see below).

However, this design and the white text against the yellow background lacked legibility. The images of the raw ingredients were rethought and omitted from the design because it seemed out of place and unappealing. Because the fonts chosen have such high contrast, the thin strokes get lost with the background rendering small text illegible. The redesign looked to solve these issues by increasing the letter strokes and eliminating the images of food.

Final Version

The revised version resolves the legibility issue by having the background split into two sections that use vibrant complementary colors. Instead of using white for the brand name, black is used instead. Now, the brand name and logo (cat) stand out against the background. The high contrast between the brand name and logo allow for differentiation between the two.

Included on each packaging flavor are the ingredients and manufacturing information (see below).

Also on each packaging is a quote (see below): "The brand you can trust to provide your cat a delicious and healthy balance of ingredients." This is the brand's promise to the consumer of quality products for their feline friend. The message was approved by a catâ€”noted by the paw print stamp.

This brand concept is very versatile and can apply to other pet foods. I designed the brand name and logo so that "well-Blend" can always stay the same as text (but change colors) and the cat logo can change pet animals to fit other applications (website, product packagings, etc) and food products. For example, if this brand expanded to include dog food, the logo would be a dog silhouette inbetween the words "well-Blend".
